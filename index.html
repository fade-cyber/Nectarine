NECTARINE is a commercial brand of confectionery and bakery, it was founded in 2007, strategically located in Lagos,Nigeria. The brand was created by Chief Miss Fadekemi Adele,who is the owner and also the chief baker,her love for baked foods led to the birth of this beautiful brand whoose top priority is catering to the baking needs of it's ever growing customers at pocket friendly prices.
After several years of hard work this small business has expanded into large bakeries and has many branches in the heart of Lagos Nigeria.
The company is proud that aesthetic and delicious characteristics of its products having gain such a high mark of approval among residents, guests and clientele not only in Lagos and its neighbouring states but worldwide.


We offer a wide range of services which include:
  • Large scale Baking for your Events and Parties
  • Cakes for Wedding and Birthday Celebrations
  • We deliver freshly baked goods to your doorsteps.
